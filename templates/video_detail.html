Additional Resources

When building your core after pregnancy, you should eat a well-balanced diet that includes nutrient-rich foods to support healing and provide energy. Here are some foods to focus on:

  • Lean proteins: Fish, chicken, nuts, and beans can help you feel full and support recovery after exercise.
  • Whole grains, fruits, and vegetables: These foods are high in fiber, which can help with constipation, and provide quick-burning energy. Fruits and vegetables also contain vitamins and minerals that support recovery.
  • Foods with omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants: These foods promote muscle recovery.
